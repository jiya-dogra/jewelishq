ABOUT US

WEAR TRANSFORM AND LOVE Jewelishq is a new age jewellery line created by JIYA DOGRA. It was setup on July 4,2022 at New Delhi. We sell hand-crafted unique designs which not only suits your taste but also celebrates you. We aim at providing lovable jewellery for every occasion. The materials used are organic and eco-friendly. The aim of our company is to minimize the waste production, and alongside achieve sustainability. Customer satisfaction is our priority and we provide the experience of custom made designs, made just for you, only to our Platinum and Black membership customers.
